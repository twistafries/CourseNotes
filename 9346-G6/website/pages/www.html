The World Wide Web

What is the world wide web?

  • invented by Sir Tim Berners-Lee in 1989
    • Father of the Web
    • Wrote the first browser program in 1990 while employed at CERN in Switzerland
  • It is an information space where documents and other web resources are identified by Uniform Resource Locators (URLs), interlinked by hypertext links, and accessed on the Internet through the web.
  • The web is basically a system of Internet servers that support specially formatted documents. A markup language called HTML allows linking to other types of documents, starting initially from text only and developing support for all other forms of media in the modern day.

What is the Internet?

  • The Internet is a global interconnected network of computing devices that uses the Internet Protocol Suite standard to serve billions of users worldwide.
  • It is a network of networks that consists of millions of private, public, academic, business and government networks of local to global scope that are linked by a broad array of electronic and wireless technologies.
  • Vinton Cerf - Father of the internet
